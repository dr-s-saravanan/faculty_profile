Academic Lectures, Keynote Addresses & Expert Sessions Delivered by Dr. S. Saravanan

Academic Contributions as Resource Person

Dr. S. Saravanan has actively contributed as a Resource Person, Chairperson, Keynote Speaker, and Expert Lecturer in various National Conferences, ICSSR Sponsored Programmes, Workshops, Research Methodology Courses, and Government Academic Events.

Resource Person Engagements

ICSSR Sponsored National Conference on Water Resource Management in India: Issues and Prospects
24–25 January 2019
Served as Resource Person in the ICSSR Sponsored National Conference organized by the Department of Economics, Thiru Vi Ka Government Arts College, Thiruvarur. Delivered a Special Lecture on "Implications of Climate Change on Water Resources in India", highlighting environmental sustainability and resource management challenges.
ICSSR Sponsored National Conference on Economic Burden of Pollution and Health in India
14–15 February 2019
Participated as a Resource Person in the National Conference organized by the Department of Economics, Periyar Government Arts College, Cuddalore. Delivered a Special Lecture on "Environmental Degradation and Economic Development in India – An Outlook".
National Level Workshop on Economic Policy, Growth, Inclusiveness and Sustainability
11 October 2019
Served as Resource Person at the National Level Workshop organized by the Departments of Economics and History, Dharmapuram Adhinam College, Mayiladuthurai. Delivered a Special Lecture on "Economic Policy, Growth, Inclusiveness and Sustainability".
TANSCHE National Level Seminar on Impact of COVID-19 on MSMEs in India
December 2021
Functioned as Chairperson for the Tamil Nadu State Council for Higher Education (TANSCHE) sponsored National Level Seminar on "Impact of COVID-19 on Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in India", organized by the PG & Research Department of Economics, Government Arts College, Kumbakonam.
Sixth Siddha Day Celebration – Government of Puducherry
08 January 2023
Participated as a Resource Person during the Sixth Siddha Day Celebration organized by the Directorate of Indian System of Medicine and Homoeopathy, Government of Puducherry.
ICSSR Sponsored Ten-Day Research Methodology Course
09 July 2023
Served as Resource Person and delivered two academic sessions in the Ten-Day Research Methodology Course sponsored by ICSSR, New Delhi and organized by the Department of Library and Information Science, Pondicherry University, Puducherry. The sessions focused on research methodology, academic writing, research design, and scholarly publication practices.
