Electoral Literacy Club Award – 2023

Year: 2023
Indira Gandhi College of Arts and Science, Puducherry

Blood Donation Award

NSS Achievement
Indira Gandhi College of Arts and Science, Puducherry
The NSS Unit of Indira Gandhi College of Arts and Science received the prestigious Blood Donation Award from Indira Gandhi Government Medical College & Research Institute (IGMCRI), Puducherry in recognition of its outstanding contribution to voluntary blood donation initiatives.

Role: Dr. S. Saravanan served as the NSS Programme Officer, coordinating and motivating volunteers for successful blood donation drives.
