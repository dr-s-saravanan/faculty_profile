Academic Profile

Dear Professor / Research Coordinator,

I am Dr. S. Saravanan, Head and Assistant Professor of Economics at Indira Gandhi College of Arts and Science, Puducherry. My academic interests encompass a wide range of areas in Economics, with specialization in International Economics and International Trade Relations.

Over the years, I have been actively engaged in teaching, research, academic administration, student mentoring, scholarly publications, conference presentations, and community-oriented extension activities through NSS and various academic forums.

At present, I am serving as an Examiner for Ph.D. Thesis Evaluation and as an External Examiner for Ph.D. Viva Voce Examinations. These responsibilities have enabled me to critically assess research quality, originality, methodology, analytical rigor, and academic contribution in diverse areas of Economics and related disciplines.

I am keenly interested in extending my academic services to Universities, Research Institutions, Autonomous Colleges, and Higher Educational Institutions across India.
