Description:

Dr. S. Saravanan participated as the Contingent Leader in the National Integration Camp (NIC) – 2023, conducted from 19 June 2023 to 25 June 2023 at B. S. Abdur Rahman Crescent Institute of Science & Technology, Vandalur, Chennai, Tamil Nadu. The camp was organized by the NSS Regional Directorate, Chennai in association with the NSS Unit of the host institution. The programme promoted national integration, cultural exchange, leadership development, community service, and collaboration among NSS volunteers from different regions of India.
